Mariza Rizou, the girl we have loved for her artistic quality, voice and creativity, as well as her humor, authenticity and disarming spontaneity, is coming to London!

In 2013, she released her first album ‘Glyko Proi’ featuring the beloved song ‘Mia alli eytychia’, where she wrote both music and lyrics, with an immediate success in the Greek radios.

In 2016, she performed ‘Petao’ with Panos Mouzourakis. The song had an even greater success and gave her the necessary boost for the release of her second album ‘Megali Giorti’, by Feelgood Records in October 2016, in which she wrote most of the songs herself. ‘Pame mia volta" and ‘Einai mikri i zoi’, have also met with big radio success.

Since 2013, she has not stopped performing live throughout Greece and Cyprus, whilst writing music for theatre.

80% light and 20% darkness applies to the character, the music, the shows and the whole worldview of Mariza Rizou. With her humor and artistic quality and truth, she has comfortably won a special place in our hearts over the years.

After her successful summer tour, she is more than ready to come to London for the first time for a unique concert!

Musicians: Angelos Aivazis - guitars / ukulele / vocals, Vangelis Katsarelis - trumpet, Panagiotis Kostopoulos - Percussion, George Mouhtaris – double bass, Markos Chaidemenos - piano / curator

Sound Editing: Agapitos Kataxakis


Opening Act - Aphrodite:

Η Aphrodite είναι μια νεα ανερχόμενη τραγουδοποιός με επιρροές από την electro-pop και alternative διεθνή μουσική σκηνή, τα τελευταία χρόνια ζει στο Λονδίνο καθώς ετοιμάζει το τρίτο της single "Fly Me Away" από το επερχόμενο αγγλόφωνο album της.
