Kostis Maraveyas: Live in London

Kostis Maraveyas returns to London this time having prepared a unique and special concert exclusively for the magnificent Union Chapel that will be reminiscent of… Greece and the moments which we are all missing dearly.

On February 24th, in one of London’s most beautiful venues, bring your friends, your smile, your stories, all your everyday things and come to spend a wonderful night together. Kostis will add the music, add the words and the notes, add the songs we love, add the feel and emotion, add the soundtrack of the night. The music of joy. And many, many trifles will create a great thing that we will all share.
