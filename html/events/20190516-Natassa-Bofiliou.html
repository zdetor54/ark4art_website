Natassa Bofiliou: Monologue

Thursday, 16 May 20:00 - 10:00
at Union Chapel

price: £33.50 | £58 VIP

Book Now
Description

Ark4Art and Prospero present Natassa Bofiliou, live in London!

Natassa Bofiliou, one of the most important Greek artists of recent years, returns to London with “Monologue”. On Thursday, May 16th, she will perform in the majestic Union Chapel.

“Monologue” is a specially designed for the Union Chapel show by Themis Karamouratidis and Gerasimos Evangelatos which includes pieces from their personal discography, original covers of famous songs from the Greek and the international music scene, as well as some unexpected surprises.

The introversion of a cathedral meets the extroversion of an explosive performer, in a cosmopolitan program that is expected to impress even the most demanding listeners.
