Ark4Art is an events and production company, registered in England and Wales and founded in February 2014.

Ark4Art is the leader in organising cultural events for the Greek diaspora and the international audience and has organised and produced more than thirty events in iconic venues, such as the Barbican Centre, Royal Festival Hall, KOKO, The Grand, The Union Chapel, the British Library and more.

Just like you, we are members of the new “diaspora”. Making a new life, making our home away from home. For us and those who come. Seeking connections. With each other. With whatever we left behind but still cherish in our hearts. We are part of a burgeoning, dynamic, expanding community. A community that increasingly needs its focal points, opportunities to get together, create new bonds.

What better way to connect people than art?

Our goal: to create little musical oases to transcend space and time, reach deep into our souls, remove boundaries, connect.
